##  Business Insights

##1. Sales peak during weekends, especially on Saturdays.
##2. Evening hours (5 PM  7 PM) generate the highest revenue.
##3. A small set of products contributes to a large portion of total sales.
##4. Quantity sold is strongly correlated with total sales value.
