## Feature Engineering
# Define pass/fail based on average score
df['average_score'] = df[['math', 'science', 'english']].mean(axis=1)

df['result'] = np.where(df['average_score'] >= 40, 'Pass', 'Fail')

##  Key Insights

#3. Attendance shows a strong positive correlation with overall performance.
#4. Failures are concentrated among students with low attendance.
