df['active_cases'] = df['confirmed'] - df['recovered'] - df['deaths']
df['recovery_rate'] = (df['recovered'] / df['confirmed']) * 100
df['fatality_rate'] = (df['deaths'] / df['confirmed']) * 100

##  Key Insights

# 1. Confirmed cases increased exponentially during the early outbreak phase.
# 2. Recovery rate improved steadily over time.
# 3. Fatality rate remained significantly lower than recovery rate.
# 4. Growth rate slowed after the initial surge, indicating control measures impact.
