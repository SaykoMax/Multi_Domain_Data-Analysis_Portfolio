Project 1: Supermarket Sales Analysis

Domain: Retail Analytics

Summary

This project analyzes supermarket transaction data to understand sales trends, product performance, customer behavior, and time-based purchasing patterns. The goal was to identify revenue drivers and operational inefficiencies.

Key Outcomes

Identified weekend and evening peak sales periods

Found that a small number of product lines contribute to most revenue

Demonstrated strong correlation between quantity sold and sales value

Impact

The insights can help businesses optimize inventory planning, staffing schedules, and promotional strategies, leading to improved revenue and customer satisfaction.

Project 2: Student Performance Analysis

Domain: Education Analytics

Summary

This project evaluates student academic data to analyze pass/fail rates, subject-wise performance, and the impact of attendance on results. The aim was to identify factors influencing academic success.

Key Outcomes

Attendance showed a strong positive correlation with performance

Mathematics emerged as the weakest subject

Students with low attendance had significantly higher failure rates

Impact

The analysis supports early intervention systems, remedial programs, and attendance monitoring to improve academic outcomes.

Project 3: Weather Data Analysis

Domain: Climate & Environmental Analytics

Summary

This project analyzes daily weather data to identify temperature trends, rainfall distribution, humidity patterns, and extreme weather events.

Key Outcomes

Detected a gradual increase in temperature over time

Found rainfall to be highly uneven, with few days contributing to most precipitation

Identified strong association between humidity and heavy rainfall

Impact

The insights are valuable for urban planning, agriculture, water resource management, and disaster preparedness.

Project 4: Stock Market Analysis

Domain: Finance Analytics

Summary

This project analyzes historical stock market data to study price trends, daily returns, volatility, and technical indicators such as moving averages.

Key Outcomes

Moving averages effectively identified trend reversals

Volatility analysis highlighted high-risk periods

Volume spikes aligned with major price movements

Impact

The analysis supports investment decision-making, risk assessment, and trading strategy development.

Project 5: COVID-19 Trends Analysis

Domain: Healthcare Analytics

Summary

This project examines COVID-19 time-series data to track confirmed cases, recoveries, deaths, active cases, and growth rates, with a focus on healthcare system impact.

Key Outcomes

Early stages showed exponential case growth

Recovery rate improved steadily over time

Growth rates declined following intervention measures

Impact

The findings assist healthcare capacity planning, public health policy evaluation, and epidemic preparedness.
